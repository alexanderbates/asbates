Open datasets I have produced or co-produced, with citations and DOIs. For the full set of BANC data locations, see the [Accessing the BANC data](/portfolio/banc-data/) walkthrough.

### Connectomes

- **BANC brain-and-nerve-cord connectome** (Harvard Dataverse) — Bates et al., 2026. [DOI: 10.7910/DVN/7WTH1N](https://doi.org/10.7910/DVN/7WTH1N)
- **BANC EM image data and segmentation** (BossDB) — Bates et al., 2026. [DOI: 10.60533/boss-2025-941r](https://doi.org/10.60533/boss-2025-941r)

### Where to browse and download the BANC

- Browse: [FlyWire Codex](https://codex.flywire.ai/banc) and the [Neuroglancer viewer](https://ng.banc.community/view)
- Download: [Harvard Dataverse](https://doi.org/10.7910/DVN/7WTH1N) (analysis-ready tables, meshes, code)
- Community hub: [banc.community](https://banc.community)
