bancpipeline is the R + SLURM pipeline that produces every compiled-data product behind the BANC paper, running on Harvard Medical School’s O2 cluster from the raw CAVE tables, synapse detections and neurotransmitter predictions.

What you can use it for. Use it as the reproducibility resource for the BANC: it assembles the per-neuron metadata and metrics, the enriched synapse tables, the edgelists, the NBLAST cross-dataset matches, the neurotransmitter predictions, and the influence shards.

Source: htem/bancpipeline. Archive: Zenodo, 10.5281/zenodo.20350571.
