Dr. Alexander S. Bates

I am a neuroscientist and programmer. I work on insect brains. I am interested in how neurons wire together, and how they work together to build complex, innate behaviours.

Originally from London, once of Cambridge, currently in Boston. I like to collaborate, exchange ideas and gossip. This website exists to help you get to know science-me.
