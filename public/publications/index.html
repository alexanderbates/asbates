Dr. Alexander S. Bates

I am a neuroscientist and programmer. I work on insect brains. I am interested in how neurons wire together, and how they work together to build complex, innate behaviours.

Originally from London, once of Cambridge, currently at Harvard Medical School in Boston. I like to collaborate and exchange ideas. This website exists to help you get to know science-me and vinegar fly brains.

“But - I thought flies didn’t have brains?” ~ Joan Bates, Grandmother, circa. Summer 2016
